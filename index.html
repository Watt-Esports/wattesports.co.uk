Heriot Watt Esports Society

The best Scottish University Society in the competitive brackets

Founded in 2017, the Watt Esports Society based in Heriot-Watt University Edinburgh has been the #1 Scottish University in the NSE standings for two years running. We run teams for video games in League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Overwatch, Rocket League, Dota 2, and more. We have a friendly community based in our discord server which is open to anyone who wants a group of friendly people to play their favourite video games with.


"This is an incredibly friendly and engaging community, I'd totally play video games with them."

Nicolas Nicolauze (Discord Manager)

Our goal is to create a safe and fun environment for our members to play video games together with no discrimination. We strive to be the best in all the competitive aspects of gaming that we can be, we do this by playing multiple teams for several games in both the NSE and NUEL championships every year. We even host friendly in-house tournaments for anyone to take part in and get involved with to increase our community outreach, also, every now and then we play varsity matches against other universities which hold Esports Societies, bringing us epic gamers together.
